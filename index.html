Data sources & method
  • Bus network — LTA stop & route data; road-aligned via OSRM. Feeders (span < 6 km) are searchable but not drawn. Dashed = express legs (no stops between).
  • Schools (337) — MOE school directory, geocoded via OneMap; footprints from OpenStreetMap.
  • IHLs (16) — curated list (universities, polytechnics, ITE, arts); campus boundaries from OpenStreetMap / Nominatim.
  • Shopping malls (237) — OpenStreetMap shop=mall, non-malls filtered out.
  • Supermarkets (532) — OpenStreetMap shop=supermarket, minimarts excluded.
  • Police (118) — OpenStreetMap amenity=police (SPF centres, posts, HQs, Coast Guard); non-SPF removed.
  • Bus hubs (54) — interchanges & terminals derived from LTA stop names. MRT/LRT lines & stations (8 lines, 182 stations) — OpenStreetMap; stations filtered to those on a drawn line (future CRL/RTS-Link stations omitted). Labels appear as you zoom in.
  • Audience — modelled opportunities-to-see = passenger taps (LTA DataMall, Passenger Volume by Bus Stops, Jun 2026) × catchment segment share (SingStat Census 2020: residents by planning area & age; households by income). Planning areas: URA Master Plan 2019. Every figure is aggregate and modelled, not panel-measured; no individual-level data (PDPA).
  • Places shown as areas where they span 2+ buildings, else as points. Within-400 m counts use footprints. All layers clipped to the Singapore boundary. Data compiled Jul 2026.
  • Basemap © OpenStreetMap contributors © CARTO.
